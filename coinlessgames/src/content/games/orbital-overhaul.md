Orbital Overhaul is a vector arcade game about cleaning up orbital debris. Shoot dead satellites into pieces small enough to tow, then haul them home on a chain that obeys physics instead of you. Inspired by Asteroids Deluxe, and by the fact that space junk is a real-world problem someone will eventually be paid to fix.

First of three Atomic Dustbin Dan games. Free, open source, runs in your browser.
