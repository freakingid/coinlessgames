A broad sweep across readability, feel, and presentation.

- **All garbage now glows one safe green.** No more guessing whether something is a threat or a pickup.
- A stronger **Magnet** and far more powerups: every UFO drops one, plus a bonus at the dock.
- Removed the leftover **dashed debug boundary**.
- **Game Over is now a spectacle:** a shockwave that sets off nearby debris, screen shake, and a white flash, instead of everything just freezing.
- Added **top-10 local high scores** with three-initial entry.
- Cleaned up the HUD layout, and gave large Hunters a slow **last-stand pursuit** so a wave can't stall out with one drifting core left.
