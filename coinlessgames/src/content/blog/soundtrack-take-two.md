The soundtrack got a full rewrite.

- Replaced the old track set with **five hand-composed tracks**: Zen, Derelict, Drift, Warehouse, and the Beacon title theme.
- **Longer loops with real melodies** and variation, so nothing wears out its welcome as fast.

Every track is WebAudio synthesis. There are no audio files anywhere in the game.
