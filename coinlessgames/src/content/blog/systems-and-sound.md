A big one. The economy got tuned, the Magnet got scary, and the game found its voice, at least musically.

- Hunter garbage drops now **scale by size**, the dock takes salvage faster, and tow capacity was raised to **24**.
- Added a behind-the-scenes **difficulty-lever framework** and a standalone scoop-tuning tool.
- **Magnet buff:** a screen-wide pull with distance falloff, double the duration, and it now grabs clumps too.
- **Low-health warning:** a looping siren plus a red pointer aimed at the nearest health pickup.
- The **music system** went live, with a proper scheduler, menu ducking, a title theme, and gameplay tracks that thicken as difficulty ramps.
