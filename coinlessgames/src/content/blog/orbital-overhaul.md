The one where the game got its name, and its identity.

- Renamed from *Asteroid Field Deluxe* to **Orbital Overhaul**.
- Replaced the asteroid-ish debris with **real wrecked-satellite line art**: six original archetypes, each tumbling on its own.
- Menu panels are **opaque** now, so you can actually read them.
- Introduced the **Scoop**, a persistent, buildable ship upgrade with five size levels that widens your salvage mouth. Take hits and you lose levels, so it's yours to earn and yours to lose.
- Weighted powerup drops, and you can now **scoop whole garbage clumps** directly instead of shooting them apart first.
