The build grew up. A real version number, a restructured menu, and for the first time, Dan talking back.

- The title screen now shows a real version: **v1.0.0.10** (Major.Minor.Patch.Changeset).
- **Dan speaks.** A synthesized voice system delivers 24 lines across eight kinds of moment: low hull, recovery, full repair, powerup pickups and expirations, big deliveries, and a full tow.
- The **low-health warning** now pulses red in **all four corners** of the screen, with a relief cue when you climb back out of danger.
- Music tracks **Drift and Warehouse gained B-sections**, and the **High Scores screen got its own fanfare**.
- Added a **ship-rotation-speed slider** under Options &rsaquo; Controls.
- Moved **Sound / Music into its own dialog**, made the High Scores board reachable from Options, and rendered it much larger.
- Reverted the Scoop to its clean **open-prong** look and removed the debug collision box around it.
