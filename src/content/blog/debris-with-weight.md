Salvage stopped being disposable and started carrying real physics.

- Garbage clumps now carry **real mass and size**. A big wad is a slow, heavy anchor that loose pieces fall into.
- A **single shot shatters a clump** back into individual pickups.
- **Salvage is now permanent.** It leaves the field exactly two ways: you recycle it, or it becomes a Hunter. The garbage economy is a closed loop.
