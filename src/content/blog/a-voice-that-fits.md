This one's being built right now, so consider it a peek at what's on the workbench rather than a finished patch.

Change Set 10 gave Dan a voice. Change Set 11 is about giving him the *right* voice, and a few more reasons to use it.

- Reworking the synthesized voice so it lands closer to the Dan aesthetic. We're adding a few distinct, unmistakably-robot voice options, with a feminine variant too. (Recorded human-Dan lines may come someday, but not in this set.)
- A real **new-level moment**: Dan calling out the next level, plus an on-screen flash or flourish so advancing feels like an event, not just the debris quietly respawning.
- Dan getting audibly **frustrated when your tow chain gets clipped** and salvage breaks loose.
- **Captions:** an optional toggle to show what's being said on screen.

More when it lands.
